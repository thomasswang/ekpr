About Us

Our company processes tons of coconut plantations spread across the continents of Indonesia.


We began our journey by collecting coconut shells from collectors/suppliers in our hometown, Jambi. There, we turn coconuts into everything worth of selling, such as copra and cocopeat. As time goes by, we began to expand our exploration of coconut to most provinces in Sumatra Continent. We started to process more derivative products of coconuts.
